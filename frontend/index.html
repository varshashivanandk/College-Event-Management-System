Why CEMS.rvu?

Everything you need to run campus events.

🗓️

Centralized Event Hub

All campus events in one place. No more hunting through WhatsApp groups or missing announcements.

✅

Faculty Approval Flow

Event proposals go through a structured approval process, keeping faculty and coordinators aligned.

🛡️

No Duplicate Registrations

The system prevents students from registering twice, keeping participant lists clean and accurate.

👥

Role-Based Access

Students, coordinators, and admins each get a tailored experience with the right permissions.

⚡

Fast & Responsive

Pages load in under 2 seconds. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers seamlessly.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Admins can monitor all events, manage users, and view registrations from a single control panel.

How It Works

From idea to event in 4 steps.

1

Create an Account

Students and coordinators register with their college credentials in seconds.

2

Create or Browse Events

Coordinators submit new events; students browse the live listing dashboard.

3

Faculty Approves

Faculty reviews the event proposal and approves it to make it visible to students.

4

Register & Attend

Students register with one click and receive a confirmation. Simple.
